BEAF BIRYANI

Layering: In a large heavy bottomed oven proof pan, arrange half of beef mixture in a single layer. Top with half of rice followed by 1 tbsp ghee,1 tsp rose water,half of saffron milk, half of fried onions, half of raisins, half of cashew nuts and 2 tbsp coriander (cilantro) and mint leaves.
