CHANA BIRYANI

Chana Pulao is a great fragrant rice dish that is a great combination of chickpeas and rice. When the chickpeas melt in the mouth it makes the dish comforting as well as satisfying. It is made to celebrate special events and holidays and it is one of the most famous dishes in Pakistani culture. It can be served with any vegetable dish, salad, raita, or plain yogurt. In Pakistan, if someone is asked what to cook and eat with rice the first thing the heart craves is Chana Pulao or Biryani as these are surely warm and comforting dishes.
