MUTTON KOFTA

If you love both Mutton & Koftas, you just have to try this quick and super easy Mutton Kofta recipe at home. If you are a fan of Mughlai recipes, you will fall in love with this creamy and flavourful Mutton Kofta. Ready in just one hour to serve to your family and friends, this mutton kofta recipe melts in the mouth and is perfect to make when you have limited time on hand. As an added bonus, you will impress everyone with your tasty and quick mutton recipe. This is cooked using minced mutton, yoghurt, gram flour and a melange of spices. This is a lip-smacking main dish recipe that you can prepare on special occasions and festivals. Those who are avid non-vegetarians would love to eat this spicy mutton recipe! You can make this yummy main dish recipe on pot lucks, kitty parties and get-togethers and people will love it for sure!
