NARGASI KOFTA

An easy-to-make side dish recipe, Nargisi Kofta is part of Mughlai cuisine which is prepared by mincing coated hard-boiled eggs in a curry sauce with the goodness of yoghurt and cashews. This non-vegetarian recipe is best served with roti, naan or rice and can be made for special occasions like kitty parties and get-togethers. This is a must try dish for all the chicken lovers.
